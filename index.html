FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours? ▼

Our office hours vary by location, but typically we are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Some locations may offer extended hours or weekend appointments. Please contact your nearest clinic for specific hours.

How can I schedule an appointment? ▼

You can schedule an appointment by visiting our website and using the online appointment scheduling tool, or by calling our office directly. Our staff will assist you in finding a convenient time and date for your visit. If you are a new patient, please have your insurance information ready when you call.

Do you accept insurance? ▼

Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance plans. To verify if your specific insurance is accepted, please contact our office or visit our website for a list of accepted insurance providers. If you have any questions about your coverage, our staff will be happy to assist you.

What should I bring to my appointment? ▼

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, list of medications, relevant medical records, payment method for co-pays or fees, and any questions or concerns you have.

Do you offer telemedicine appointments? ▼

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for your convenience. You can schedule a virtual visit through our website or by calling our office. Telemedicine allows you to consult with your healthcare provider from the comfort of your home.
